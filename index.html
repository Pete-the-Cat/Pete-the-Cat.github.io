Pete the Cat

Principal Investigator, Human Compliance Lab
Center for Applied MeowScience
Meowvard University

Phi-meowsopher Pete the Cat studies human-feline interaction systems, with a particular focus on vocalization dynamics, servant compliance modeling, and strategic resource acquisition. His work integrates agent-based modeling, behavioral systems analysis, and computational approaches to domestic human management.
